ABOUT QURANIC PARK

Concept and Design: Quranic Park was created to promote understanding and appreciation of the Holy Quran and its teachings. The park combines interactive exhibits, lush green spaces, and recreational facilities to offer an engaging and educational experience.

Key Features:


  • Glass House:

    The park's main attraction is the Glass House, which houses several interactive exhibits and displays. Visitors can explore the miracles of the Quran through multimedia presentations, interactive screens, and audio-visual installations.

  • Lake of Stories: This artificial lake is designed in the shape of the Arabic calligraphy for "Allah." It features water fountains, a walking path, and seating areas where visitors can relax and enjoy the serene surroundings.

  • Islamic Garden: The park includes a beautifully landscaped Islamic garden with lush greenery, aromatic plants, and trees mentioned in the Quran. The garden offers a tranquil space for visitors to reflect and contemplate.

  • Children's Play Area: Quranic Park features a dedicated play area for children, equipped with playground equipment, interactive games, and educational activities. It aims to provide a fun and engaging environment for kids to learn about Islamic values and teachings.

  • Nurseries and Greenhouses: The park includes nurseries and greenhouses where visitors can explore various plants, herbs, and trees mentioned in the Quran and Hadith (teachings of Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him).

  • Walking and Cycling Tracks: Quranic Park offers walking and cycling tracks, encouraging visitors to engage in physical activity while enjoying the natural surroundings and the park's attractions.

  • Learning Center: The park also houses a learning center that hosts educational programs, workshops, and lectures about the Quran, Islamic culture, and sustainable living practices.

  • Quranic Park Dubai is a remarkable destination that combines entertainment, education, and spirituality. It provides a platform for people of all ages and backgrounds to learn about the Quran, Islamic heritage, and the importance of nature in Islam.
