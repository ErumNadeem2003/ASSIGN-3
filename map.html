Location: Quranic Park is situated in the Al Khawaneej area of Dubai, near the Dubai Safari Park. The park covers an area of approximately 60 hectares (about 148 acres).

Entry to Quranic Park is free of charge for all visitors. The park operates from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays (Sunday to Wednesday) and from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM on weekends (Thursday to Saturday). For more information you can visit this website: KHALEEJ TIMES
